Setup Instructions

1
Create Slack App

Go to api.slack.com/apps -> "Create an App" -> "From scratch".

2
Enable Webhooks

In the sidebar, find "Incoming Webhooks" and toggle it to "On".

3
Add to Workspace

Click "Add New Webhook to Workspace", choose a channel and copy the URL.
