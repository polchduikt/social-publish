How it works

Three simple steps to automate your social media pipeline.

1. Create your content

Use our beautifully designed, distraction-free editor. Add emojis, format your text, and preview how it will look on different platforms before you publish.

2. Schedule accurately

Select your desired date and time using our 24-hour localized time picker. Whether it's tomorrow morning or next month, we've got you covered.

3. Relax and let us handle the rest

Our resilient backend takes over. If a network error occurs while posting to Telegram or Discord, our intelligent retry system ensures your post will eventually go through.
